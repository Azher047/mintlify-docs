## Delivery methods

Specter delivers data however your workflow demands:

- **Web platform** — Search, track, and analyze directly in the Specter app
- **REST API** — Enrich your CRM, power internal tools, or build custom sourcing workflows
- **Chrome extension** — Surface insights as you browse LinkedIn or any company website
- **Bulk data** — Ingest entire datasets via AWS S3, Google Cloud, or Snowflake
- **Specter MCP** — Talk to your AI and get Specter-powered intelligence inline
