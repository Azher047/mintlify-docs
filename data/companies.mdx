## Natural Language Search
You don't need to build complex filter stacks. Describe what you're looking for in plain English:
> *"Show me growth stage AI video companies"*
> *"Find B2B SaaS companies in the Nordics with 50–200 employees that raised a Series A in the last 18 months"*
Specter maps your query to the right filters and returns a ranked list of matching companies — no manual filter configuration required.
## Similar Company Search
Use the **Similar Companies** feature on any profile to surface competitors, adjacent players, and potential acquisition targets that share a company's market positioning. It's useful for:
- **Competitive intelligence:** map a market segment before a deal closes.
- **Sourcing alternatives:** find comparable companies when your primary target isn't available.
- **Market sizing:** see all the meaningful players in a space in one view.
## API endpoints
Everything above is available through the Companies API. Full request and response detail for each endpoint lives in the [Companies API reference](/api/companies).

| Method | Endpoint | Returns |
|---|---|---|
| `POST` | `/companies` | Enrich a company by domain, LinkedIn, or Crunchbase URL |
| `GET` | `/companies/search` | Find a company by name |
| `GET` | `/companies/{companyId}` | A full company profile by ID |
| `GET` | `/companies/{companyId}/similar` | Similar companies |
| `GET` | `/companies/{companyId}/people` | People at a company |
| `GET` | `/companies/{companyId}/funding-rounds` | A company's funding rounds |
| `GET` | `/companies/{companyId}/acquisitions` | A company's acquisitions |
| `GET` | `/companies/{companyId}/ipos` | A company's IPOs |
| `POST` | `/companies/by-id/bulk` | Enrich up to 50 companies in one request |
| `POST` | `/companies/similar/by-id/bulk` | Similar companies for up to 50 seed companies |
