## Natural Language Search
You don't need to build complex filter stacks to find the right companies. Specter's natural language search lets you describe what you're looking for in plain English:
> *"Show me growth stage AI video companies"*
> *"Find B2B SaaS companies in the Nordics with 50–200 employees that raised a Series A in the last 18 months"*
> *"List climate tech startups with strong web traffic growth and a recent leadership hire"*
Specter interprets your query, maps it to the relevant filters, and returns a ranked list of matching companies — no manual filter configuration required.
## Similar Company Search
Use the **Similar Companies** feature on any profile to instantly surface competitors, adjacent players, and potential acquisition targets that share the same market positioning. This is particularly useful for:
- **Competitive intelligence:** Build a complete map of a market segment before a deal closes.
- **Sourcing alternatives:** If your primary target isn't available, find comparable companies at similar stages.
- **Market sizing:** Understand the density of a space by seeing all meaningful players in one view.
## API Reference
Retrieve a full company profile programmatically using the company's unique identifier.
```bash
GET https://app.tryspecter.com/api/v1/companies/{companyId}
```
**Headers**
| Header | Value |
|---|---|
| `X-API-Key` | Your Specter API key |
**Example Request**
```bash
curl -X GET "https://app.tryspecter.com/api/v1/companies/comp_01hx9z3kp2" \
  -H "X-API-Key: sk_live_yourApiKeyHere"
```
