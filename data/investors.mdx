Each investor profile goes beyond basic directory data: you get fund-level details, a structured view of their portfolio, historical investment patterns, and — critically — **interest signals** that surface which investors are actively evaluating new opportunities before deals are ever announced.

Whether you're a founder researching the right lead for your next round, an investor benchmarking peers, or an advisor mapping the LP landscape, Specter's investor intelligence gives you the context to engage with precision.

## Interest Signals: Know Before the Deal

Specter monitors publicly observable signals to identify when investors are **actively in deal-sourcing mode** — even before any term sheet is signed or announcement is made. These signals include:

- **Sector engagement:** Increased activity around companies in a specific vertical
- **Stage clustering:** Recent investments concentrated in a particular funding stage
- **Portfolio gaps:** Sectors or geographies underrepresented relative to stated thesis
- **Team hiring:** New investment professionals being added in a specific focus area
- **Content & event signals:** Speaking engagements, published theses, and conference attendance patterns

<Note>
  Interest signals are updated continuously and are most actionable when combined with company-level tracking. For example, set up a watchlist of target investors and receive alerts when their interest signals align with a company you're monitoring.
</Note>

## API Reference

Retrieve a full investor profile using the investor's unique Specter identifier.

```bash
GET https://app.tryspecter.com/api/v1/investors/{investorId}
```

**Headers**

| Header | Value |
|---|---|
| `X-API-Key` | Your Specter API key |

**Example Request**

```bash
curl -X GET "https://app.tryspecter.com/api/v1/investors/inv_09qt5r2lc1" \
  -H "X-API-Key: sk_live_yourApiKeyHere"
```
