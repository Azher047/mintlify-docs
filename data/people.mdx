Specter's people database spans **550 million+ professional profiles** — covering founders, executives, investors, operators, and emerging talent across every industry and geography. Each profile aggregates **60+ data points** sourced from public signals, career networks, and proprietary intelligence to give you a complete picture of the individuals behind the companies you're tracking. Use people data to evaluate founding teams, map networks, identify warm introduction paths, or surface talent signals before a hire or investment.

## Founder Highlights

Specter applies a set of AI-generated **founder highlight flags** to surface exceptional backgrounds during team due diligence or sourcing. These signals are available as filters in search and are returned in the API response.

| Highlight | What It Means |
|---|---|
| `Prior Exit` | Founded or co-founded a company that was acquired or had a successful exit |
| `Prior IPO` | Part of a founding or executive team that took a company public |
| `Serial Founder` | Has founded two or more companies, regardless of outcome |
| `VC-Backed Founder` | Previously ran a venture-backed company |
| `Top University` | Attended a globally recognized institution (e.g., MIT, Stanford, Oxbridge, IITs) |
| `Unicorn Experience` | Worked at or founded a company that reached a $1B+ valuation |
| `Fortune 500 Experience` | Held a significant role at a Fortune 500 company before founding |

<Note>
  Founder highlights are particularly powerful when combined with company filters. For example: *"Show me Series A companies in fintech where at least one founder has a Prior Exit and a Top University background."*
</Note>

## API Reference

Retrieve a full people profile using a person's unique Specter identifier.

```bash
GET https://app.tryspecter.com/api/v1/people/{personId}
```

**Headers**

| Header | Value |
|---|---|
| `X-API-Key` | Your Specter API key |

**Example Request**

```bash
curl -X GET "https://app.tryspecter.com/api/v1/people/per_04jw2m8bn7" \
  -H "X-API-Key: sk_live_yourApiKeyHere"
```
