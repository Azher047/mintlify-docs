Talent Signals track the people movements that matter most in private markets — before a new company is announced, before a funding round is disclosed, and before a hire shows up in a press release. Specter surfaces 1,000+ talent signals every week, covering everything from founders spinning out of unicorns to stealth hires at portfolio companies you already back. Because the best deal opportunities are often hiding inside a career pivot, Talent Signals give you a systematic way to catch them.

## Using Talent Signals in Your Workflow

<Steps>
  <Step title="Choose your signal categories">
    From the Signals page, open the Talent Signals feed and select the categories relevant to your investment or sourcing strategy. You can subscribe to one category or combine multiple filters.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Layer in additional filters">
    Narrow your feed by industry, geography, previous company, or founder highlights (such as "Prior IPO" or "VC-Backed Founder") to reduce noise and focus on the profiles most relevant to your thesis.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save your search">
    Save the filtered view as a named search. Specter will automatically push new matching talent signals into your feed as they are verified — typically within hours of the underlying event.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Connect to your CRM">
    Push new talent signals directly to your CRM as contacts or deal leads. Specter enriches each record with the person's full profile, career history, and associated company data.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## API Reference

Query Talent Signals programmatically to integrate people-movement intelligence into your own data pipelines, prospecting workflows, or portfolio monitoring tools.

### Endpoint

```
GET /api/v1/signals/talent
```

### Query Parameters

| Parameter | Type | Description |
|---|---|---|
| `type` | `string` | Signal category: `new_founder`, `stealth_founder`, `yc_founder`, `spinout_founder`, `fortune500_spinout`, `portfolio_hire`. |
| `from_date` | `string` | ISO 8601 date. Returns signals on or after this date. |
| `to_date` | `string` | ISO 8601 date. Returns signals on or before this date. |
| `highlight` | `string` | Filter by founder highlight tag, e.g. `prior_ipo`, `vc_backed_founder`, `prior_acquisition`. |
| `industry` | `string` | Filter by industry vertical, e.g. `fintech`, `ai_ml`, `healthcare`. |
| `limit` | `integer` | Maximum number of results (default: `50`, max: `500`). |

### Example Request

```bash
curl -X GET "https://app.tryspecter.com/api/v1/signals/talent?type=new_founder&from_date=2024-01-01" \
  -H "X-API-Key: YOUR_API_KEY" \
  -H "Content-Type: application/json"
```

### Response Fields

| Field | Type | Description |
|---|---|---|
| `person_id` | `string` | Specter's unique identifier for the person. |
| `person_name` | `string` | Full name of the individual. |
| `signal_type` | `string` | Category of the talent signal (see query parameter values above). |
| `previous_company` | `string` | The company the person was at before the signal event. |
| `new_company_or_role` | `string` | The new company name, role, or "Stealth" designation. |
| `signal_date` | `string` | ISO 8601 date the signal was verified and published. |
| `highlights` | `array` | AI-generated tags summarizing the person's career pedigree. |
