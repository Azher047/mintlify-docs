The Specter Chrome extension brings your private markets intelligence layer directly into your browser. Whether you're visiting a company's website, reviewing a LinkedIn profile, or doing competitive research, you can instantly surface Specter's enriched data without switching tabs. From the extension, you can also push records straight to your CRM, add companies or people to lists, or like them to save for later — all without leaving the page you're on.

## Installing the Extension

<Steps>
  <Step title="Visit the Chrome Web Store and search for Specter">
    Open Google Chrome and go to the [Chrome Web Store](https://chrome.google.com/webstore). Search for **"Specter"** to find the official extension.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Add to Chrome">
    On the Specter extension page, click **Add to Chrome** and confirm the installation when prompted. The extension installs in seconds.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Pin the extension and log in">
    Once installed, click the puzzle-piece icon in your Chrome toolbar and pin Specter for easy access. Click the Specter icon and log in with your Specter account credentials.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Browse to any company website or LinkedIn page">
    Navigate to a company website or LinkedIn profile. The Specter extension automatically detects the company or person and surfaces relevant insights from Specter's database directly on the page.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## What You Can Do from the Extension

### View Company and Person Profiles

The extension overlays enriched Specter data — funding history, headcount, growth signals, investor relationships, and more — directly on the page you're viewing. There's no need to copy a company name and search separately; the context is right there.

### LinkedIn Integration

When you visit a LinkedIn company page or individual profile, the extension surfaces the matching Specter record inline. You'll see funding details, recent news signals, headcount growth, and key executive information directly alongside LinkedIn's native content — giving you a richer picture of who you're looking at before you reach out.

### Push to CRM

Found a company worth tracking? Click **Push to CRM** in the extension to send the record directly to your connected CRM (Attio, Affinity, or Salesforce). Specter pre-fills the record with enriched data so you're not starting from scratch.

### Add to Lists

Add any company or person to a Specter list directly from the extension. This is useful for building shortlists during research sessions without interrupting your browsing flow.

### Like Companies

Click the like button on any company to save it to your liked companies in Specter. Liked companies are easy to revisit and can be used as the basis for saved searches or CRM pushes later.

<Tip>
  The extension works on any public website, not just LinkedIn. If you land on a startup's homepage, a portfolio page, or a news article mentioning a company, Specter will attempt to match and surface the relevant record automatically.
</Tip>
