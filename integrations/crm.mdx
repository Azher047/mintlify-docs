## How It Works

### Pull (Sync) — Enrich Your CRM

When you sync Specter with your CRM, Specter enriches every matched record with hundreds of data points: funding history, investor relationships, headcount growth, technology stack, key hires, news signals, and more. For example, you can enrich 2,000 existing leads with the latest funding rounds, headcount trajectory, and senior executive profiles — all without leaving your CRM.

### Push — Add New Records from Specter

Found a promising set of companies or people in Specter? Push them directly into your CRM as new records or add them to existing lists in bulk. This keeps your prospecting workflow tight: search in Specter, qualify, and push to CRM in a single flow.

### Automation — Stay Up to Date

Connect a saved search in Specter to your CRM sync and any company or person that matches your criteria in the future is automatically added and enriched. Your CRM evolves alongside the market without any manual upkeep.

## Connecting Attio

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Integrations in your Specter dashboard">
    From the left sidebar, navigate to **Integrations**. You'll see all available integration options listed here.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select Attio and click Install">
    Find the Attio card and click **Install**. This begins the connection flow between Specter and your Attio workspace.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Authenticate with your Attio account">
    You'll be redirected to Attio to grant Specter the permissions it needs. Log in and authorize the connection.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Map Specter fields to your CRM properties">
    Once connected, map the Specter data fields you want — funding stage, headcount, revenue signals, job titles, and more — to the corresponding properties in your Attio workspace.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enable auto-sync on saved searches">
    Head to any saved search in Specter and toggle on **Auto-sync to Attio**. From this point forward, any new results matching that search are pushed to your CRM automatically.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  Automation requires at least one saved search to be configured in Specter. Without a saved search, records will only sync on demand. Set up your first saved search under **Search** in the Specter dashboard.
</Note>

<Tip>
  Start with a tightly scoped saved search — for example, Series A–B SaaS companies in North America with 20%+ headcount growth — to keep your CRM focused on the highest-signal opportunities.
</Tip>
