When your use case goes beyond individual searches or CRM enrichment, Specter's Bulk Data delivery gives you direct access to the underlying datasets at full scale. Whether you're building a data warehouse, training a machine learning model, powering internal tooling, or running large-scale analytics, Specter can deliver complete, structured datasets to your infrastructure on a schedule that fits your workflow.

## Delivery Options

Specter delivers bulk data to the cloud infrastructure you already use. There's no proprietary format or custom tooling required — data lands in your environment and is ready for your existing stack.

<CardGroup cols={3}>
  <Card title="AWS S3" icon="aws">
    Receive datasets as structured files dropped into your S3 bucket on your agreed delivery schedule.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Google Cloud Storage" icon="google">
    Deliver data directly into a GCS bucket in your Google Cloud project for seamless integration with BigQuery and other GCP services.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Snowflake" icon="snowflake">

</CardGroup>

## Available Datasets

Each dataset is kept current and delivered with consistent, documented schemas so your pipelines stay reliable.

| Dataset | Scale | What's Included |

| **People** | 550M+ records | Professional profiles, career history, contact data, affiliations |
| **Investors** | 330K+ records | VC firms, angels, family offices, investment history and focus |

## What You Can Build

- **Data warehousing** — maintain a live copy of Specter's universe in your own infrastructure for full control and flexibility
- **BI dashboards** — connect Specter datasets to Tableau, Looker, Power BI, or any SQL-compatible tool
- **ML and AI training** — use Specter's structured company and people data as training inputs for your own models
- **Custom analytics** — join Specter data against your internal CRM, portfolio data, or proprietary datasets to generate unique insights
- **Internal tooling** — power prospecting tools, scoring models, or portfolio monitoring systems with a continuously refreshed Specter data feed

## Getting Started

<Steps>
  <Step title="Contact Specter sales to discuss your data needs">
    Reach out to the Specter team to explain your use case, the datasets you need, and your preferred delivery target. The team will scope the right configuration for your volume and schedule.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Specter provisions access to your chosen delivery target">
    Once your requirements are agreed, Specter sets up the delivery pipeline — whether that's granting write access to your S3 bucket, configuring a GCS transfer, or setting up a Snowflake data share.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Data is delivered on your agreed schedule">
    Depending on your plan and use case, Specter delivers data on a daily, weekly, or real-time streaming basis. Each delivery includes updated records and, where applicable, incremental change sets.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Connect to your analytics stack">
    With data in your environment, wire it into your existing tools — query it in Snowflake, load it into BigQuery, run it through your ETL pipeline, or read it directly from S3.
  </Step>
</Steps>
