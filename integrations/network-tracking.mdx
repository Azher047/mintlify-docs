Your team's collective network is one of your most valuable sourcing assets — but it only creates value when you can actually see it. Specter's Network Tracking integration connects your professional accounts and consolidates your team's relationships into a single, searchable view. When a contact switches roles, launches a company, or attracts investor interest, you hear about it first. And when you're trying to reach someone at a target company, you can see exactly who on your team already knows them.

## Data Sources

Specter Network Tracking connects with the tools your team already uses every day:

- **LinkedIn** — sync your professional connections and get alerts on career moves and new ventures
- **Gmail** — surface relationship context from your email history to understand how well you know a contact
- **Outlook** — the same email relationship intelligence for Microsoft 365 users
- **Calendar** — meeting history adds another signal of relationship strength, helping Specter understand who you've actually spent time with

## Features

### Comprehensive Syncing

Specter doesn't just pull from a single account. When multiple team members connect their accounts, Specter combines all of those connections into one unified network view. A contact known by your partner may be the warm intro your associate needs — now that's visible to everyone.

### Expanded Team Visibility

Every team member who connects their accounts adds to your collective network map. The result is dramatically expanded coverage: overlapping connections are deduplicated, and the full combined reach of your team is surfaced in a single place.

### Real-Time Insights

Specter monitors your connected contacts for meaningful changes and delivers personalized signals when something noteworthy happens — a contact joins a new company, a founder you backed raises a round, a person in your network launches a startup, or a former colleague's company attracts institutional investment. These signals appear in your Specter feed so you can act on them at the right moment.

### Enhanced Relationship Mapping

For any target company, Specter shows you who on your team knows someone there and how strong that connection is. Instead of cold outreach, you can identify warm introduction paths and reach out through the right person — making your outreach more effective and more personal.

## Connecting LinkedIn

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Integrations > Network Tracking in Specter">
    From the left sidebar in your Specter dashboard, navigate to **Integrations**, then select **Network Tracking**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Connect LinkedIn">
    Find the LinkedIn card and click **Connect LinkedIn** to begin the authorization flow.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Authorize Specter to read your connections">
    You'll be redirected to LinkedIn to grant Specter read access to your connections. Review the permissions and click **Allow**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Your connections sync and become searchable">
    Once authorized, Specter begins syncing your LinkedIn connections in the background. When the sync completes, your contacts are searchable in Specter and contribute to your team's unified network map.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  Ask every member of your team to connect their LinkedIn accounts. The more connections that feed into the network, the more warm introduction paths Specter can surface when you're prospecting into a new company.
</Tip>

## Finding Warm Introduction Paths

Once your team's accounts are connected, navigate to any company profile in Specter and look for the **Relationships** panel. Specter shows you which team members have connections at that company, the strength of each relationship, and the specific people they know. You can use this directly to coordinate outreach or request an introduction through Specter.
